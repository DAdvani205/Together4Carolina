Mission

Founded by Indian American leaders and supporters, Together for Carolina seeks to channel the energy, generosity, and volunteer spirit of our community into meaningful local impact — helping create healthier families, stronger communities, and greater opportunity across North Carolina.

Our mission is to mobilize our community and local partners to bring meaningful, measurable, and lasting change to families that are too often overlooked. Our focus is on providing healthy food access, basic healthcare and education support.

We believe real impact happens when communities come together with focus, compassion, and long-term commitment. Rather than spreading resources thinly across many regions, Together for Carolina is built around a simple idea: focus on a township, build trusted local partnerships, create measurable outcomes, and scale thoughtfully over time.

Approach

Healthy food access first

We are beginning with healthy food access because nutrition is foundational to long-term health, educational success, and economic stability.

Through partnerships with local nonprofits, schools, healthcare providers, volunteers, and community organizations, we aim to support families with healthier meals, nutrition-focused initiatives, and community-based support programs.
